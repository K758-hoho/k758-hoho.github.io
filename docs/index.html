Dive Into The Story...

[UNDER DEVELOPMENT]

Tapio Boskval, a former enforcer from the Strigoi crime family turned restauranteur, runs Hava Rosso, a struggling illegal drug operation disguised as a cozy roast bar. All Tapio wants is a quiet life after years of violence, but peace is shattered when his youngest recruit, Rikki, sparks a dangerous gang war. Now, Tapio must confront vengeful enemies from his brutal past, while Rikki fights to redeem himself. Can they survive in a world gone mad?

Created by Karin Kho, this webcomic is currently under development, with plans to release Season 1 consistently every week upon completion. To stay updated, subscribe to Target Boskval by logging in to Comicfury or by subscribing to the RSS Feed.
